National Association of Disabled Wives, Nepal (NADWN) was established in September 22, 2008 by a group of women with disabilities and without disabilities. NADWN is legally registered at District Administration Office (DAO), Kathmandu and affiliated with Social Welfare Council (SWC) on October 31, 2008.The people with disabilities in Nepal are highly disadvantaged-first by poverty, and then by social and economic exclusion. Overcoming the hurdles facing people with disabilities is often challenging in Nepal.
